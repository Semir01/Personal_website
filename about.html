About me

Hello, I’m Semir and this is my story so far

I’m an engineer by nature and a software enthusiast by passion. I was born 30 january 2001 in Sarajevo and grew up in the beautiful town of Glavatičevo, where my curiosity for technology and innovation began at an early age.

Throughout elementary and high school, I developed a deep interest in electrical engineering, research, and innovation. As the valedictorian of OŠ Glavatičevo, and later a student of the Electrical Engineering High School, specializing in Power Engineering. I built a strong foundation of knowledge and skills. From childhood, working alongside my brother and father, I gained over 10 years of hands-on experience in electrical installations, both low-voltage and high-voltage systems

In 2020, I started my studies at the Faculty of Information Technologies in Mostar. I initially enrolled in Software Engineering and later transferred to the Software Development program. Today, I’m a senior student, combining my two biggest passions, electrical engineering and IT. Over the years, I’ve also worked in two companies, where I further expanded my technical, professional, and teamwork skills.

I’m currently focused on advancing my career in the IT field, especially in Full-Stack Development, with the goal of creating projects that combine hardware and software, innovation, and real-world application.
